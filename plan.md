# Packd - Your Breezy Beach Planner
*(Replacing the previous project scope)*

## 1. Project Overview
Packd is a specialized travel planning application designed for anyone who wants to travel. The goal is to simplify the often-stressful process of planning a trip by providing a visually inspiring, easy-to-use platform that captures the relaxing essence of a beach getaway.

## 2. Design Philosophy: "Azure Drift"
The visual identity is built on the Azure Drift design system, characterized by:
*   **Color Palette:** A refreshing mix of deep ocean blue (`#0077B6`), sunny yellow accents, and crisp, airy whites.
*   **Typography:** Modern and legible (Plus Jakarta Sans) to maintain a premium yet approachable feel.
*   **Aesthetics:** Soft shadows, rounded corners (8px), and high-quality coastal imagery to evoke a sense of calm and excitement.

## 3. Target Audience
*   **Anyone Who Wants to Travel:** Designed to be universally accessible and inspiring for all types of travelers—whether they are meticulous organizers planning every hour, or casual explorers seeking quick destination inspiration.

## 4. Key Features & Screens

### 4.1. Landing Page (Inspiration)
*   **Hero Section:** High-impact coastal imagery with a simplified search/planner entry.
*   **Trending Destinations:** Curated "top picks" to spark interest.
*   **Value Propositions:** Clear communication of "Effortless Planning," "Verified Stays," and "24/7 Support."

### 4.2. Explore Destinations (Discovery)
*   **Smart Search:** Ability to search by city, island, or beach name.
*   **Activity Filters:** Tailor results by surfing, snorkeling, relaxation, or sailing.
*   **Logistics Filters:** Filter by price range and travel time (distance).
*   **Destination Cards:** Quick-view snippets with ratings, pricing, and location.

### 4.3. Trip Dashboard (Overview)
*   **Current Trip Highlights:** Prominent display of the upcoming destination and a countdown timer.
*   **Weather Integration:** Real-time forecast for the destination.
*   **Interactive Map:** Visual representation of saved spots and points of interest.
*   **Quick Timeline:** A snapshot of the Day 1 and Day 2 itinerary.

### 4.4. Itinerary Builder (Planning)
*   **Activity Library:** A curated list of potential activities with drag-and-drop functionality.
*   **Daily Schedule:** Granular time-slot planning (e.g., 09:00 AM - Arrive & Check-in).
*   **Collaborative Tools:** Options to share the plan and save it for offline access.

## 5. Success Metrics
*   **Engagement:** Time spent exploring destinations.
*   **Conversion:** Number of finalized itineraries created.
*   **Retention:** Users returning to their Dashboard for active trip management.

## 6. Future Roadmap
*   **Booking Integration:** Seamless booking of flights and stays directly from the itinerary.
*   **Social Sharing:** Allow users to publish their "Paradise Guides" for others.
*   **Mobile App:** A companion app for real-time navigation.
